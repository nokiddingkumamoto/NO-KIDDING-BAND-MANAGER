NO KIDDING BAND MANAGER V4.0.3 upload guide

1. Extract this ZIP on your phone.
2. Upload all extracted files and the assets folder to the root of the GitHub repository.
3. Overwrite index.html, styles.css, app.js, sw.js, VERSION.txt and UPLOAD_GUIDE.txt.
4. Wait about 1-2 minutes, then reopen the GitHub Pages URL.
5. If the old design remains, close the browser tab completely and open it again.

Do not upload the ZIP itself.
